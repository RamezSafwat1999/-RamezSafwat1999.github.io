Canal Walk Island

Where Living and Nature Blend Seamlessly

205’s unwavering aim to exceed all expectations and transform Egyptian real estate, this project introduces Canal Walk. It’s design is the first-of-its kind for Egypt, pioneering waterfront landscaping, reminiscent of the beauty of Venice and the charm of Amsterdam. These elements justly give novel meaning to luxury waterfront living. The Canal Walk has been born from a belief in providing not just exemplary homes, but exemplary lifestyles. It offers unobstructed views along all residences, with the homes specifically designed to accentuate the project’s exceptional topography.


Tower Point

The 45th Floor And Only Skies Above You

Three majestic towers will transform West Cairo’s skyline. With its sleek, modern and intricate design, this imposing 150 meter cluster comprises of three forty-six story high-rises. These iconic pieces of architecture boast the sense, taste and talent of its creators.


Retail + Leisure

Limitless Retail Possibilities, In One Place

Look no further than 205 should you care to indulge in some retail therapy. Offering a combination of upscale retail stores provided by a multitude of local and international fashion boutiques, complimented by select casual and fine dining establishments, 205 caters to all.


Business District

The Perfect Enviroment For Business To Thrive

205 will effectively become the ‘central business district’ for West Cairo, providing an optimal workplace typology that promotes connectivity and engagement.


El Safa Med Park

State-of-the-Art Medical Complex

205 comprises of a world-class, state-of-the art medical complex providing highly personalized head-to-toe clinical care. This facility offers patients a very wide range of expertise and treatment under one roof, provided by leading medical professionals in their specialist fields.


Hospitality

A Great Stay Is Closer Than You Think

Hospitality is an integral element of 205, boasting 3 hotel operators within its walls. All hotels in the development have been configured in a highly efficient manner and provide world-class amenities and facilities to suit, each hotel space leaves you wanting for nothing.
